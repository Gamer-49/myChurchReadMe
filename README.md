# myChurch — Community & Administrative Platform

> An administrative and public engagement platform designed for modern community management and ease of use.

---

## 📖 Overview

**myChurch** is a full-featured digital platform built to serve churches and faith-based communities of all sizes. It bridges the gap between church administration and the congregation by providing tools that simplify everyday operations while keeping members connected, informed, and engaged.

This repository serves as a **public summary** of the private development project. No source code is shared here — only information about what is being built and why.

---

## 🎯 Purpose & Vision

Managing a church involves far more than Sunday services. From event coordination and member communication to volunteer scheduling and giving records, the administrative workload can be overwhelming. **myChurch** was designed to:

- Reduce manual administrative overhead for church staff and leaders
- Give congregation members a single, easy-to-use place to stay connected
- Enable transparency between leadership and the community
- Make modern church management accessible regardless of technical skill level

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🏛️ Administration Panel
- **Member Management** — Add, update, and organize member profiles and family groups
- **Attendance Tracking** — Record and review attendance for services and events
- **Event Scheduling** — Create, manage, and publish events with RSVP support
- **Volunteer Coordination** — Schedule and notify volunteers for roles and responsibilities
- **Announcements & Broadcasts** — Send targeted communications to the full congregation or specific groups
- **Financial Giving Records** — Track tithes and donations with reporting tools (no payment processing built in)
- **Document Storage** — Store and share important church documents securely

### 🌐 Public & Member Engagement
- **Member Portal** — Members can log in to view their profile, giving history, and upcoming events
- **Event Calendar** — A publicly visible calendar of services, events, and community activities
- **Sermon Library** — Archive and share sermon notes, audio links, or video embeds
- **Prayer Requests** — Submit and view community prayer requests
- **Small Groups / Ministries** — Browse and join available ministry teams and small groups
- **News & Devotionals** — Share daily devotionals, news, and inspirational content

### 🔒 Security & Access Control
- Role-based access for Admins, Staff, Volunteers, and Members
- Secure authentication with session management
- Private member data kept separate from public-facing content
